                              ENVIRONMENTAL MONITORING AND REGULARIZATION


                              Spatial analysis carried out through field surveys and remote sensing to identify different forms of land use in 350 rural properties in the Transamazônica region, for the purposes of preparing the Rural Environmental Registry, Payment for Environmental Services and Licensing of Rural Activities.

                                SPATIAL ANALYSIS


                                The spatial analysis of the demographic census is crucial in geomarketing, allowing companies to identify areas of high market potential, understand the demographic profile of consumers and optimize location and advertising strategies.

                                  FIRE MONITORING


                                  The mapping of fire scars from the MapBiomas Fogo project in Brazil was based on image mosaics from Landsat satellites with a spatial resolution of 30 meters. The mapping period was from 1985 to 2022, with monthly and annual data on fire scars covering the entire Brazilian territory.

                                    Analysis of the Burned Area in Brazil


                                    Interactive application that brings together consolidated data on the spatial and temporal patterns of wildfires across Brazil’s biomes and other territorial categories from 1985 to 2024, highlighting the main land cover and land use classes affected and the frequency of fire throughout the country.

                                      Natural Regeneration Potential


                                      Multicriteria variables such as pasture age, slope, elevation, fire frequency, distance to forest edges, distance to water bodies, distance to roads, and land use and land cover classes were used to identify areas with the highest potential for natural regeneration within the Apyterewa Indigenous Land. Integrating these factors made it possible to analyze the combined influence of terrain, anthropogenic pressures, and disturbance history on vegetation recovery processes, providing essential support for planning management and conservation actions in the territory.

                                        Election Results


                                        Application developed to visualize the distribution of votes by polling location for the mayoral election in Altamira-PA in 2024. The project organizes and presents the total votes received at each voting site in an interactive way, making it possible to identify spatial patterns, areas with higher voter concentration, and comparisons across different electoral zones.

                                          Demographic and Socioeconomic Data


                                          3D map application displaying detailed demographic and socioeconomic data for the state of Rio de Janeiro by census tract (setor censitário), based on the latest IBGE census.

                                            Geoprocessing course - Indigenous Land


                                            Geoprocessing course conducted for the community of the Apyterewa Indigenous Land, aimed at strengthening local capacities in the use of GIS tools, mapping, and territorial monitoring. The training covered fundamental concepts of digital cartography, collection and interpretation of geospatial data, and practical applications focused on tracking land use, identifying degraded areas, and supporting the environmental management of the territory.

                                              Drone Survey for Urban Land Regularization


                                              Aerial survey activity using drones carried out to identify urban properties and support land regularization processes. The mapping generated high-resolution images that allow for precise delineation of lots and buildings, making it easier to identify occupations and update cadastral records. This data is essential for urban planning and for formalizing property ownership.

                                                Property Registration


                                                Development of a Geographic Information System (GIS) for property registration. The platform includes a range of features such as search by owner, address, or property number, user access control, and a comprehensive data backup system. This tool streamlines the management of real estate records, ensuring organized, secure, and easily accessible cadastral information.

                                                  Analysis of Census Data


                                                  Analysis of IBGE census data for the municipality of Barueri, focused on identifying demographic, socioeconomic, and territorial characteristics to support the planning of marketing actions. The study provided insights into population profiles, income distribution, age groups, and other key indicators, enabling the design of targeted campaigns, prioritization of areas, and development of customized communication and service strategies.

                                                    Urban Property Information


                                                    WebGIS application developed for the visualization and consultation of urban property data, allowing interactive access to cadastral information, geographic location, and parcel characteristics. The tool streamlines territorial monitoring and management by offering filters for owners, addresses, areas, and other attributes, as well as enabling report generation and supporting urban planning and land regularization processes.
